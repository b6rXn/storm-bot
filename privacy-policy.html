## Privacy Policy StormBot respects your privacy. This bot collects only the data necessary to provide its features, which may include: - User IDs for giveaways, invites, and leaderboard tracking. - Role and channel information needed for role management or welcome messages. **Data Usage:** - Data is stored securely in a local database and is used solely for the bot's functionality. - No personal data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties. **Data Retention:** - Giveaway entries, messages counts, and invite data are kept only as long as necessary for active bot operations. - Users can request data deletion by contacting the bot's support team. By using StormBot, you consent to the collection and usage of this data as described above.
